About SerøCore

SerøCore is more than a website — it's an archive of untold truths, raw voices, and unheard human stories. Built by Achamo Opara, a soul forged in resilience, this platform stands to give voice to those who never had one.

Born from the fire of the Anuak genocide, SerøCore is a place for rebirth — for finding strength in silence and purpose in pain.

This is just the beginning. Stories live here now. Stories that matter.
